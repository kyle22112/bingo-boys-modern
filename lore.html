Brodie

A child named Eidorb showed his mother a drawing he had created, but she crumpled it up and threw it away. Eidorb grew up to hate all forms of creativity and created an army of what he called the Crodies. The Crodies ruled the world for years, until a Crodie named Grug was born with creativity. Grug grew up and started an army called the Brodies to fight the Crodies and spread creativity. The war still goes on to this very day.
